What I Do?

Haptics & Industrial Tele-robtics

I specialize in Haptics Teleoperation and Industrial Tele-Robotics, leveraging these skills in an intercontinental experiment conducted between the Haptics Human-Robotics and Condition Monitoring Lab (HHRCM) at NEDUET, Karachi, Pakistan, and the Interactive Graphics and Simulation Group (IGS) at the University of Innsbruck, Austria. Through this experiment, I pioneered a groundbreaking approach to teleoperate robotic devices seamlessly across continents, showcasing the integration of haptic technology with industrial tele-robotics. These skills enable me to design immersive and responsive interfaces, facilitating remote operations with near-real tactile sensations, vital for various industries and research domains.

Haptic Virtual Environments

In the realm of Haptics Virtual Environments, my expertise lies in constructing immersive worlds that transcend the digital realm by merging advanced haptics technology with virtual environments. This proficiency was exemplified through projects where I orchestrated environments that provided lifelike sensory feedback, fostering an experience where interactions within the virtual space felt tangibly real. These endeavors transcend conventional simulations, enabling comprehensive training modules for professionals like doctors, engineers, and various specialists, ensuring an unparalleled blend of realism and training convenience.
